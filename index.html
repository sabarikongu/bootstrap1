Welcome to Continental AG

Continental develops pioneering technologies and services for sustainable and connected mobility of people and their goods. Founded in 1871, the technology company offers safe, efficient, intelligent and affordable solutions for vehicles, machines, traffic and transportation.

Provide world-class Engineering Education, Foster Research and Development. Evolve innovative applications of Technology. Encourage Entrepreneurship. Ultimately mould young men and women capable of assuming leadership of the society for the betterment of the Country.
